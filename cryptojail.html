WHAT IS CRYPTOJAIL?

CryptoJail is not just a game; it's a fun way that we build a digital economy where you take the reins of your own carceral empire. Built on the speed and efficiency of the Solana blockchain, CryptoJail immerses you in a virtual jail, where you take profit from the prisioners. Build. Collect. Eearn.

Your mission is clear: acquire prison floors in NFT format, each with its own capacity. Then, populate these cells with unique NFT inmates, which vary in level and profit-generating capability. The more high-level inmates you manage, the more $JAIL tokens you will produce.

Wisely manage your custody chain, expand your empire floor by floor, and dominate the blockchain underworld as your inmates work for you! It's time to turn punishment into profit and strategy into digital wealth.

CryptoJail: Where strategy meets decentralization, and your digital assets generate financial freedom.

YOUR JOURNEY IN CRYPTOJAIL

Discover the steps to build and expand your empire in CryptoJail. A well-defined strategy is the key to success.

  1. 1. Acquire Your First Prison Floor (NFT)

    Your journey begins with the purchase of an NFT prison floor. This is your primary asset, the foundation of your empire.

    • Initial Price: The first floor has a fixed cost of 20 USDC.
    • Capacity: Each initial floor has a capacity for 2 NFT Inmates.
    » Each floor is a unique NFT on Solana, ensuring your ownership and transferability. Think of it as your digital real estate in the game!

  2. 2. Populating Your Cells with NFT Inmates

    NFT Inmates are the soul of your prison, responsible for passively generating $JAIL tokens.

    • Income Generation: Inmates are acquired using the $JAIL token. The cost varies by level.
    • Productivity: Higher-level inmates cost more $JAIL, but in return, generate a significantly larger amount of $JAIL over time.
    » Strategically choose your inmates to optimize $JAIL production and maximize your profits.

  3. 3. Expand Your Empire Floor by Floor

    To unlock the purchase of a new floor, you must have completely occupied all inmate slots of the previous floor. There are no shortcuts; expansion requires effective management.

    • Price Scaling: The price of each new floor is double the price of the previous floor.
    • Capacity Scaling: The inmate capacity of each new floor is the previous floor's capacity plus 1.
    • Progression Examples:
      • Floor 1: 20 USDC | Capacity: 2 Inmates
      • (After filling Floor 1)
      • Floor 2: 40 USDC | Capacity: 3 Inmates
      • (After filling Floor 2)
      • Floor 3: 80 USDC | Capacity: 4 Inmates
    » The vicious cycle of profit: more inmates, more $JAIL, more expansion!

  4. 4. Generate and Utilize $JAIL

    Once your inmates are in their cells, they begin to passively generate $JAIL tokens for you continuously.

    • Passive Income: Use the generated $JAIL to buy more higher-level inmates, invest in new floors (indirectly), or trade it on the market.
    » The cycle of wealth: the more prisoners, the more $JAIL, the greater the expansion!
