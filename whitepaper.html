🏁 SUI Cars – Whitepaper

🚗 Overview

SUI Cars is a next-generation Web3 racing game built on the Sui blockchain. Players own unique pixel-art cars as NFTs, race them daily, and earn rewards based on rarity and strategy. No complex attributes — just clean, fast, and scalable fun.

🔥 Core Features

  • NFT Ownership – Every car is an on-chain NFT you can trade, collect, or race.
  • Three Rarities:
    • ⭐ Common – Standard cars, easily mintable
    • ⭐⭐ Hybrid – Rarer, with enhanced visual appeal
    • ⭐⭐⭐ Electric – Premium gold-themed collectibles
  • Daily Racing & Rewards – Cars automatically enter daily simulated races. Payouts are based on rarity and randomness.
  • On-Chain Game Logic – Smart contracts ensure transparent gameplay and rewards on the Sui network.
