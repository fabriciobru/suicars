F.A.Q.

How do I participate in the Airdrop?

Participation is easy! First, connect your SUI wallet using the button on this page. Then, start completing the available tasks listed above to earn points.

How are points calculated?

Points are awarded for completing specific actions, such as following our social media, joining our community channels, or interacting with the SUI Cars platform. Each task has a designated point value.

How often is the ranking updated?

The ranking is updated approximately every hour to reflect newly earned points and task completions.

How do you verify social media tasks?

Verification may require linking your social media accounts (like X/Twitter or Discord) to your Airdrop profile. Our system will then check if the required actions have been performed. Some tasks might involve manual review.

When will the Airdrop rewards be distributed?

The exact distribution date for the Airdrop rewards will be announced closer to the official launch of the $SCAR token (TGE).

What kind of rewards can I get?

By participating and earning points, you qualify for a share of the $SCAR token airdrop. Top participants may also receive additional bonuses like NFT whitelist spots.

Is there a referral system?

A referral system may be introduced later in the campaign. Please stay tuned for updates on new ways to earn points!
